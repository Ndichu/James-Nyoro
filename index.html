Manifesto

James Nyoro Manifesto

Rebuild a fairer local economy

Our raisin detre is to transform Kenya into a modern nation with an attractive quality of life and a strong economy that is business and development focus. We are firmly aware that this transformation requires strong man and woman of courage to provide firm and strong leadership

Improve the Quality of health services and Lower the Cost

Kenya needs a holistic care system that is universally accessible, affordable and effective and drastically reduces the out of pocket spending on health. We accords high priority to health sector, which is crucial for securing the economy.

Fight against Corruption

Corruption has been tolerated in Kenya for too long. It distorts our economy, frustrates our progress and damages our reputation. It is time to get tough on those who seek to use their positions of power for their own personal gain.We will clean up Government by introducing some of the toughest anti- corruption legislation in the world.

Next Generation Infrastructure

We will: • Set up Gas Grids to make gas available to households and industry. • Critical development of Gas Field and explore other gas fields • Set up a National Optical-Fibre Network up to the village level; and Wi-Fi zones in public areas. • Harness satellite technology and expertise for development.

+

Improve Electricity and water distribution infrastructure

Ensure every Kenyan has access to renewable source of energy and fresh reliable water by 2025. Encourage and develop projects and create an enabling environment based on international best practices to both attract private enterprise and speed up the delivery of energy projects for the benefit of the people of Kenya. Encourage new private energy generators in the Kenyan market and increase private investment in existing state-owned companies such as Kenya power and Kengen.

Agriculture modernisation and productivity

Agriculture is the engine of nation’s economic growth and the largest employer, and we commits highest priority to agricultural growth, increase in farmer’s income and rural development. we will: • Increase public investment in agriculture and rural development. • take steps to enhance the profitability in agriculture • The cost of production, cheaper agriculture inputs and credit; introducing latest technologies for farming and high yielding seeds.

Nationalist-Kenyan
5/5

He worked as Managing Director at the Rockefeller Foundation before venturing into politics. In 2013, Nyoro ventured into politics, unsuccessfully, contesting for the Kiambu governor seat. He lost the seat to former Kiambu governor William Kabogo. In 2016, Nyoro was appointed as an advisor to the government on matters Agriculture and Policy.

He was attached to the Deputy President office. In the run up to the 2017 polls, Nyoro declared interest to unseat former governor Kabogo. He, however, dropped his bid in support of Governor Ferdinand Waititu after an agreement under the United for Kiambu Alliance.

James Nyoro Seed distribution in over 60 wards in Kiambu

The Foundation has now given out more than 23,000 water tanks since its inception in the year 2006. The tank beneficiaries include the youth operating car washes, children’s homes, churches, schools and police posts/stations among others. The Foundation is anchored on three pillars: Free water storage tanks, Free computer & IT colleges and Free community service vans. Thousands of students have gained computer and IT skills and knowledge from the colleges, and tens of thousands have directly or indirectly benefited from the community service vans that come fitted with a sound system, fully fuelled and with a driver.

distribution of maize and beans seeds in all our 60 Wards in Kiambu

Another 2000 farmers in Kamangu, Nachu and Lusigetti have today become the latest beneficiaries of our continued distribution of maize and beans seeds in all our 60 Wards in Kiambu. I also distributed food items to cushion some of our most vulnerable people. I also took time to inform the residents about the projects that my administration has implemented in these areas that include water connection to over 20,000 residents, road rehabilitation and upgrading to bituminous standards and improvement of health facilities. I urged them to elect leaders in the coming General Election based on their development track record.

The Karuri and Kiambu Municipality road projects

I have commended the Department of Municipalities and Urban Development for its technical support towards the construction of roads and other public amenities in our urban areas, which has gone a long way in opening up the road network in Kiambu County. I have launched the Karuri and Kiambu Municipality road projects namely: The 1.5km Equity - Slaughter house road, the 400M cabro-paved Muchatha shopping centre loop road in Mucatha Ward, the Kiambu County Headquarters loop road and the stepped footpath connecting Kiambu town to Indian Bazaar at Kagoya from Rumathi through Garage in Kiambu Township Ward. Similarly, I have launched the rehabilitation of rural access roads under the roads maintenance program in Ting'ang'a ward, Kiambu Sub-County. I strive to make our urban areas more habitable and accessible to spur economic growth.

James Nyoro- Kiambu title deed project

7,000 land owners in Ndeiya, Karai and Nachu Wards in Kikuyu Sub County are set to benefit from title deeds. This follows the ongoing verification and validation of 40,000 acres of Ndeiya and Karai arable scheme. My administration will ensure that in the next one month, the genuine alottees will have title deeds. This one week exercise is being conducted in conjunction with the National Lands Commission and Ministry of Lands and Physical Planning. I am urging all Ndeiya and Karai arable scheme owners to visit County Headquarters this week with ;- original and copy of title deeds, letter of allotment and a copy, national ID and a copy and any other proof of ownership. These title deeds will contribute to the growth of these areas as owners can now develop their land, sub divide and seek development approvals. My administration is committed to uplifting the standards of life of the people of Kiambu.
